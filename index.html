Child Symptom Checker

Abstract

Parents in the UK find it difficult to identify early signs of illnesses in their children, often leading to cases where children die of preventable causes. The purpose of our web-app prototype is to provide parents of young children with video supported information resources to help understand signs and symptoms of acute illnesses so that they can spot when their child is sick and take the necessary actions
